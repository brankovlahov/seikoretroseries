"Always ahead of the others"

This has been always the key message to the Seiko founder , Kintaro Hattori. This message has inspired our company to go over a century of watchmaking. Our quest to provide precision, utility and beauty and on top of that a better environment for everybody has resulted in "Seiko" being a pioneer and setting standards for many brands. For example in 1963 we brought the Seiko Quartz Astron it was the world's first quartz watch. In the year 2012 we gave the world the first GPS solar watch that immediatly set standars in markets worldwide. As we always strive for a better world from this year we are launching our "Seiko" vintage series from recycled materials. We hope that this series will warm the hearts of our older and new generation of fans and that it will be a spark of a hope for better future.

Shinji Hattori Chairman and CEO
