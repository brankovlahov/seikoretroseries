Why Recycling Matters

Even though people think that recylcing doesn't make that much of an impact in the big scheme of things, they are absolutely wrong. Recycling matters and it matters a lot! The more we recycle the more we save valuable resources. This will help us and aswell as future generations. It is economic and practical and it allows for maximum usage of the planet resources. Recycling allows us to reuse resources we already have, therefore we are not extracting new materials from our already low on resource planet earth. That's why we at Seiko decided to jump into the recycling and bring back the iconic vintage Seiko watches this time made from 100% recycled materials.
