Seiko 6138


Image source https://www.fratellowatches.com

Some of the coolest vintage chronograph

The seiko 6138 is a chronograph watch. Chronographs from this era are known for their colorful accents, interesting case shapes, and a pretty innovative mechanical movements. This series were affordable watches at the time when they were relased, and as a result they were often not treated with much care. An interesting fact about this watch is that all parts and movements are created in the house of "Seiko" not a lot of companies can do this even today. The Seiko 6138 is restored and polished and it is ready to shine on your wrists again.
