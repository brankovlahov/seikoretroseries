Seiko 6106


Image source https://www.fratellowatches.com

A closer look at the first sports diver!

This model was introduced in the year 1968, and was made only for a couple of years. Even though it was a popular choice for the surfers, marketing ads were boosting its diving capability saying that it can go down up to 229 feet! It was made in a number of colors: dark grey, white/gold, navy blue and dark green. It came with a stainless bracelet but the watch itself can look also good on very different straps. We can say that the Seiko 6106 is a beauthiful classic for a number of reasons and we are proud to have it back so you can discover those reasons by yourself.
