Seiko 5625


Image source https://www.fratellowatches.com

King Seiko is back in action!

Our objective with this watch was simple, to produce a beauthful looking watch with that will create the most accurate movements. After an absence of 10 years it is finally back and enthusiasts can't wait to have it on their wrists. Originally made with a bit of an expensive price tag, but thanks to our recycling philosophy nowadays you can get it for an affordable price. The case has a typical (King)Seiko shape and this is what makes it so special. On the back of the watch you can see a KS medallion in the center and aswell as the serial number along with the production year and month.
