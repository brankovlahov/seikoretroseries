Seiko 6309


Image source https://www.fratellowatches.com

Affordable vintage style.

This stainless steel wrist watch continues to be an absolute favorite to all Seiko collectors. Part of it is recycled from old fishing nets taken from deep sea levels. This beauty of a watch was used between the years 2000-2005 and is returning in fashion nowadays aswell. There is quite an amount of stories and examples from people that this series of Seiko can last working up to 30+ years. Like his previous predecessor 6105 the 6309 has also been shown in the pop culture. Ed Harris had it in the film "The Abyss" and also Mick Jagger was photographed wearing a 6309. We are very proud to have this gem back in bussiness. This series of Seiko comes up with 20 years of warranty.
