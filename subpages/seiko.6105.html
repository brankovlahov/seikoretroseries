Seiko 6105


Image source https://www.fratellowatches.com

The definitive Seiko diver!

Our brand has a rich history in the world of dive watches, and it's safe to say that this model is one of the most iconic ones. It is a stainless steel, self-winding watch that is tested up to 492 ft. underwater. The build quality and the finishing is absolutely exceptional. One nice touch on this model is that is not often found in other Seiko watches is that the movement hacks which means the second hand stops when the crown is pulled out. What is special about the crown of this watch is that when it is in a locked state it can't be moved at all so the only way to adjust time and date is to pop out the crown.
