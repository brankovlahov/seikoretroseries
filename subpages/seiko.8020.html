Seiko 8020


Image source https://www.fratellowatches.com

The panda inspired design.

The "Panda" Seiko as most people call it was made in 1969 a big year for the watch making history. And it saw the development of 3 chronograph watches. It is said that it is one of the most beauthiful vintage Seiko watches. It has 2 dial combinations and a beauthiful color. The watch itself is 41 mm in diameter. It has a very simplistic design which is the main reason why it became so famous among the vintage "Seiko" collectors. Since it gained popularity around various collectors forums in the year 2019 we decided to return the "Panda" so you can be a proud owner aswell.
