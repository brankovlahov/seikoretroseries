Seiko 7001


Image source https://www.fratellowatches.com

Your best self-wind watch choice.

The Seiko 7001 is 40mm men's automatic watch, it is a water resistant watch up to 150 meters so its a perfect choice for your deep diving adventures. This watch runs up to 40 hours when its in a full wound, and it charges with self-wind when moved. The crown screws help to prevent the watch from being accidentaly pulled out while being under water. This watch is recycled from old Seiko watches and as we care about sustainablity the rubber band can be removed and replaced with another band when its worn out.
