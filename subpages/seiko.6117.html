Seiko 6117


Image source https://www.fratellowatches.com

An affordable vintage.

The seiko 6117 is a GMT watch meaning that it has a functioanlity to add a second time zone. Mainly used from pilots, bussiness people and hard travellers. The GMT proved to be a useful tool for many reasons. Bussiness people from Vegas for example can know the current time in Switzerland and vice-versa. This world-time was made circa February 1972, and has a beauthiful grey dial with a subtle linen texture finished with a red 24 hour circle. Recycled and back in action this beauty is ready to follow your adventures wherever the wind takes you.
